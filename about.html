Our mission is to make volunteering and helping easier for everyone.

The Los Angeles Community Support website is a tool for volunteers looking to make an impact in the community and offer a helping hand to those in need.

Read our story

Our founding

This website was created because of a need for young people to get involved in Los Angeles. Many young people want to help with volunteer oppurtunities so this website was made to connect organizations and helping hands.

Programs & Oppurtunities

The city of Los Angeles has oppurtunities with programs like LA Regional Food Bank, LA Mission, and A Place Called Home. These organizations help families in need with a steady food supply, house the homeless as well as mental health counseling for the youth.
