    Get Started

    Start building by making an impact in the community.

    Keep the Community Clean

    This initiative in Los Angeles is to keep the community clean by starting a weekend clean up day. Every weekend we choose a location in Los Angeles to pick up trash and beautify the city.

    Environment Project

    Keep the community green by teaming up to plant trees around the city. Once a month we gather together to do some landscaping at our parks or at local community gardens.

    Homeless Outreach

    Every week we give volunteers an oppurtunity to partner with LA Mission to feed the homeless. The oppurtunity can range from distributing donated food to handing out toiletries.

    Get Involved with Organizations

    Organizations like A Place Called Home is a great place to volunteer and be a big brother to a youth. Organizations such as APCH keep the youth off the streets by introducing them to arts and sports.
